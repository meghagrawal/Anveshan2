Message From CEO

Welcome to IGDTUW Anveshan Centre (IAC), Delhi’s newly established business incubator in the region of Delhi. Whether you’re starting with just an idea or looking for ways to grow, we are committed to your long-term success and offer a wide range of solutions and resources to help you start and grow an innovative and viable business. Our foundation is a section 8 company promoted by Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women to create an entrepreneurial culture, promote start-ups an intellectual property creation that can lead to the value creation, employment for inclusive and sustainable development.

I urge all the students, scholars and alumni of IGDTUW, who desire to start their journey towards entrepreneurship as a budding entrepreneur and want to pursue what they love with passion to turn challenges into opportunities to take all the benefits of our business incubation centre.

Your contribution and hard work may not only fulfil your dreams but it may also be an important factor to scale up national interest.

Your success is our success.


-Dr. V K Arora

(Chief Executive Officer)
